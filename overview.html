Overview

Sheet Ninja is a powerful synchronization tool that bridges the gap between Google Sheets and GitLab, allowing you to manage your project tasks seamlessly across both platforms.

Sheet to GitLab

Create GitLab issues from your Google Sheets data with automatic mapping and validation.

GitLab to Sheet

Update your Google Sheets with GitLab issue IDs and status information.
